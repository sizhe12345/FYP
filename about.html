About Population of Numbers

Population of Numbers is a website that visualizes data. Through Google Maps, we frame the counties of the country and state and distinguish them by age, gender and race.



                                                   





"Replacing digital data with visual data" is our mission.




"Population of Numbers," a reference to a book called "Book of Numbers," Israel's first census document. "In the second month of the second year after they came out of Egypt, on the first day of the second month, the LORD spoke to Moses in the Tent of Meeting in the wilderness of Sinai, saying: from the age of twenty, whoever is able to go out to fight in Israel: you and Aaron shall number them according to their army... Numbers 9:1"
